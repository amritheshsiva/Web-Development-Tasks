Sports News — Asia Cup 2025: India‑Pakistan Clash and What to Expect

The Asia Cup 2025, held in the United Arab Emirates from September 9 to 28, promises some of the most thrilling cricket matchups in recent memory. Among these, the India vs Pakistan clash on September 14 is expected to be the marquee match.


Here are some of the things fans are watching for:

  1. Group A dynamics — India, Pakistan, UAE, and Oman are grouped together.
  2. The possibility of multiple India‑Pakistan meetings, in the Super 4 stage or even the final.
  3. How teams like UAE and Oman will fare — often underdogs, but capable of surprises.
