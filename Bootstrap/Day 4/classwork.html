FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions


How can I reset my password?
To reset your password, click on "Forgot Password" on the login page, and follow the instructions. You’ll receive an email with a link to create a new password.
What are your support hours?
Our support team is available Monday to Friday, from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM (your local time), excluding public holidays.
Can I change or cancel my subscription?
Yes, you can change or cancel your subscription anytime from your account settings under the “Billing” section. Changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
