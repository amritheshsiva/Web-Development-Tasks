Capturing the Golden Hour:Tips for Stunning Photos

by Morgan Sage

Photography is all about seeing the world through a different lens.Understanding light and composition can transform an ordinary scene into something extraordiary.The golden hour - the first hour after sunrise and the last hour before sunset - is perfect for creating warm,vibrant images.

Experimenting with angles and perspectives helps your photographs stand out.Don't just shoot from eye level; try crouching low, shooting from above, or using reflections to add depth.
